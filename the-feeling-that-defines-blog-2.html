The Feeling that defines #JaiHind

When it comes to your country, the place where you live, the place which you must be proud of, you say,"I've my own rules".
Its okay. Its totally okay. I'm not here to judge anyone neither I've the right to do so. But I don't know why but I've always a feeling that my elders have taught me.
In Indian culture, from the first day itself a child is taught to love and respect his/her mother, father and elders and everyone else. We proudly consider our land, our country as mother. It's our culture and I totally don't feel ashamed rather I feel proud at my such beautiful Indian Culture.
India is not just a country, it is emotion and a feeling that we Indians are proud to have.
Now, you must be wodering about why I am writing all this stuff instead of writing some tech related stuff. So, here is your answer, I'm writing all this to remind myself that I can never be a person who can consider his country as just a mere country.
So, what happened today, I was talking with a person on whatsapp. In some context, when I texted 'Jai Hind', I got a sad emoji in reply.
I felt broken, really shattered and let me be clear that thsi feeling was not because of that person in general.
Sometimes I have a feeling that, is this the same India that Netaji had imagined, probably not.
I have only one thing to say to those people who feel proud in saying that how beloved foreign things are, how much they aspire to get a job abroad and then appreciate about that place. I am not saying that that are bad thing but isn't is a shame to not to hail for your country which provided you the guts to even dream about.
So that's it.
If you feel like I am wrong at some point, feel free to talk to me by scheduling a meeting on calendly.
Lets talk in another blog.
